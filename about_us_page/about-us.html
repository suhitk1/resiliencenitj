About Us

Hello everyone !
Here is a group of people from 2016-20 batch of NIT J; trying something new, striving to contribute to NITJ’s campus life in totality and to set a common narrative.

We are building an E-MAGAZINE called RESILIENCE.

The idea behind the this is to bring the best of all happenings about NITJ at a single platform of our budding magazine & blog. One area of our delicate focus is helping the upcoming freshers because very few people know what is going on and off in our college. We bet it’s going to be a great idea and for this we will need your help , together we will create this , bit by bit .We have also made a short campus video which can be accessed here.

Any sort of help is appreciated . Help us canvass the True NITJ & to bring a change to the Round & Round clock like life of an Engineer!We have many exciting thoughts down the lane!

For suggestions & ideas please connect to us at the contact section!
